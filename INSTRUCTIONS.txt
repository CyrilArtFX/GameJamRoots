[Instructions]
You have to use (grab & rotate) mirrors to reflect lights sources to the braziers to find your way to the tree room.

- Move using [Keyboard WASD] or [Left Joystick]
- Look using [Mouse] or [Right Joystick]
- Grab using [Left Mouse Button] or [Gamepad X/East Button]
- Moving while Grabbing pull/push objects in your direction
- Rotating Left while Grabbing using [Keyboard A] or [Gamepad Left Trigger]
- Rotating Right while Grabbing using [Keyboard E] or [Gamepad Right Trigger]
